# EPISODE 1 - "A Fat Bald White Man"

**Heatmap peaks:** 00:00 (1.0), 29:22 (0.39), 17:37 (0.34)

**Task 1 (Prize Task) -- ~02:00**
- **Type:** Prize
- **Description:** "Bring in the most interesting autograph on the most interesting vegetable."
- **Summary:** Contestants brought signed vegetables. Highlights: Mel brought Take That signatures on various vegetables; Noel brought David Suchet's autograph on a broad bean (winner); Hugh brought "Malcolm X" on a carrot (last).

**Task 2 -- ~07:30**
- **Type:** Solo
- **Description:** "Destroy this cake. Most beautiful destruction wins. You have 30 minutes. Your time starts now."
- **Summary:** Each contestant was given a cake and had to destroy it in the most aesthetically beautiful way. Hugh went sinister with knives and made a clock; Lolly made a miniature scene.

**Task 3 -- ~17:30**
- **Type:** Solo
- **Description:** "Draw this person. You must not look directly at the person you are drawing, or communicate with them in any way other than asking questions to establish their name. Most accurate drawing wins."
- **Summary:** A model named Jennifer Christine Wright sat for them, but they couldn't look at her directly. Noel won with the best drawing. Hugh famously looked via a mirror and drew "a fat, bald white man" despite the model being a black woman.
- **CROWD FAVORITE** -- Heatmap peak at 17:37 falls during this task.

**Task 4 -- ~29:00**
- **Type:** Solo
- **Description:** "Fell all the rubber ducks. Fastest wins. Your time starts when the first rubber duck falls."
- **Summary:** Contestants had to knock over all rubber ducks from behind a rope. Joe used elaborate setup with a bowling ball. Noel wore a skeleton/nuclear suit and the ducks "thought he was their leader."
- **CROWD FAVORITE** -- Heatmap peak at 29:22 falls during this task.

**Task 6 (Live Task) -- ~40:00**
